Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

Yes, our office is open to visitors during operating hours, but we recommend calling ahead to ensure someone is available to assist you.

Do you offer emergency support?

Yes, for GBV-related emergencies, please call our emergency line immediately. For other emergencies during off-hours, leave a message and we'll respond as soon as possible.

Is the contact form secure?

Yes, all information submitted through our contact form is encrypted and kept confidential.
