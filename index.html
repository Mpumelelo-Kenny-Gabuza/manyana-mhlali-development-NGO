Our Mission

To be a social enterprise that strives to live the values, principles, and practices of good governance and human rights. We are committed to building a community that tackles its own traditional, social, health, and economic challenges.

Eliminate Violence

To eliminate violence including gender-based violence

Enhance Quality of Life

To enhance the quality of life for women, children and vulnerable groups through mental health programmes

Strengthen Rite of Passage

To strengthen the rite of passage through safe initiation processes

Address Economic Factors

To address economic factors through sustainable livelihood programmes

Survivor Story

I lost my voice to fear, trapped in my own home for years. The silence was deafening. Reaching out to MMD's support group was the hardest step I ever took, but it was my first step toward freedom. They provided a safe space where I was heard without judgment and given the tools to rebuild my life. Today, I stand tall, my future is my own, and my voice, once silenced, now speaks with strength and hope.
