Building Sustainable Livelihoods

Empowering our community through skills, opportunity, and economic independence

Transforming lives by creating pathways out of poverty through sustainable employment and entrepreneurship

Our Vision for Economic Empowerment

At Manyana Mhlali Development, we believe that sustainable livelihoods are the foundation of a thriving community. Our programme is designed to break the cycle of poverty by equipping community members with practical skills, entrepreneurial knowledge, and the resources needed to build self-sufficient futures.

We focus on creating not just jobs, but sustainable careers and businesses that can support families for generations to come.
