Safe Male Circumcision Programme

Honoring Tradition, Ensuring Safety, Building Healthy Futures

Respecting Culture, Protecting Health

At Manyana Mhlali Development, we bridge the vital connection between respected traditional initiation practices and modern medical safety. Our programme ensures that this important rite of passage is conducted with the highest standards of health, safety, and cultural dignity.


We work closely with families, traditional leaders, and healthcare professionals to create a supportive environment where young men can undergo this tradition safely and return to their communities as healthy, respected members.

Why Safe Practices Matter

Health Benefits

  • Reduced risk of HIV and STI transmission
  • Improved hygiene and personal care
  • Lower risk of urinary tract infections
  • Decreased chance of penile cancer
  • Professional wound care and healing

Cultural & Personal Benefits

  • Preservation of important cultural traditions
  • Meaningful rite of passage experience
  • Community recognition and acceptance
  • Enhanced self-esteem and identity
  • Stronger connection to cultural heritage

Our Safe Process

1

Community Engagement & Education

We meet with families and traditional leaders to explain our health and safety protocols, ensuring everyone understands and supports the medically supervised approach.

2

Health Screening & Preparation

Each participant receives a thorough health screening to ensure they are medically fit for the procedure. We discuss what to expect and how to prepare.

3

Safe Procedure & Monitoring

Conducted in a clean, safe environment by trained healthcare professionals using sterile equipment and proper medical techniques.

4

Aftercare & Support

We provide comprehensive aftercare instructions, follow-up checks, and support throughout the healing process to prevent complications.
